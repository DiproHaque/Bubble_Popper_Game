# 🌶️ Spicy Bubble Popper 🔥

A fast-paced, addictive bubble-popping game with **combos**, **golden bubbles**, and **dangerous bombs**! Test your reflexes and see how high you can score in 30 seconds.

![Game Preview](https://via.placeholder.com/600x400?text=Spicy+Bubble+Popper+Demo)

---

## 🎮 Game Features

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **💙 Normal Bubbles** | +1 point × combo multiplier |
| **⭐ Golden Bubbles** | +5 points × combo multiplier (rare!) |
| **💣 Bomb Bubbles** | -3 points & breaks your combo streak |
| **⚡ Combo System** | Pop bubbles within 1.5 seconds to build multiplier |
| **📈 Dynamic Speed** | Bubbles spawn faster as your score increases |
| **⏱️ 30-Second Timer** | Beat the clock for high score |
| **📱 Mobile Friendly** | Works with touch and mouse |

---

## 🕹️ How to Play

1. **Click or tap** on bubbles to pop them
2. Pop bubbles **quickly** to build your combo multiplier
3. **Golden bubbles** give BIG points when combo is high
4. **AVOID bombs** — they ruin your score and combo
5. Survive **30 seconds** and get the highest score possible!

---

## 🔥 Combo System

```
Pop #1 → 1x multiplier
Pop #2 within 1.5 sec → 2x multiplier
Pop #3 within 1.5 sec → 3x multiplier
... and so on!
```

- Combo resets if you wait too long between pops
- Bombs reset your combo to zero AND deduct points
- Golden bubbles become **extremely valuable** at high combo

## 🧠 Game Design Highlights

- **Canvas-based rendering** for smooth animations
- **Dynamic spawn rate** — game gets harder as you score more
- **Overlap prevention** — bubbles never spawn on top of each other
- **Combo window tracking** — precise timing mechanics
- **Visual feedback** — color changes, messages, and animations for every action
